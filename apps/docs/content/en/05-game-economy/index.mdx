# Dual Currency System

The Mahjong Stars economy is designed to balance accessibility with sustainability. By employing a dual currency system and carefully structured tokenomics, the platform accommodates different types of players and investors while maintaining long-term viability.

<Callout type="success">
**Best of Both Worlds**: The dual currency system allows casual players to enjoy the full experience with soft currency, while enabling blockchain enthusiasts to participate in governance and trading through tokens.
</Callout>

---

## Soft Currency (In-Game Credits)

Soft currency is either purchased or earned primarily through gameplay and functions as the day-to-day currency of Mahjong Stars. It is accessible to all players without any blockchain or cryptocurrency knowledge required.

### Primary Uses

**Core Gameplay**:
- **Character Purchases**: Buy characters from the primary store
- **Charm Purchases**: Acquire charms and cosmetic items
- **Tournament Entry Fees**: Enter casual tournaments and competitions
- **Raffle Tickets**: Purchase entries for charm/character raffles

**Progression & Upgrades**:
- **AI Training**: Accelerate AI companion learning
- **AI Modules**: Purchase specialized training modules
- **Character Upgrades**: Level up characters faster
- **Skill Resets**: Reallocate skill tree points

**Customization**:
- **Studio Decorations**: Customize your virtual space
- **Theme Packs**: Unlock visual themes
- **Table Customization**: Personalize game tables
- **Cosmetic Items**: Pure aesthetic purchases

<Callout type="info">
**Fully Functional**: Soft currency enables complete gameplay experience. No token purchases are required to enjoy Mahjong Stars.
</Callout>

### Earning Soft Currency

Players can earn soft currency through multiple channels:

**Primary Earning Methods**:
- **Match Victories**: 50-500 soft currency per win (based on stakes and difficulty)
- **Match Participation**: 10-50 soft currency even for losses
- **Daily Quests**: 500-2,000 soft currency per day
- **Weekly Challenges**: 2,000-10,000 soft currency per week
- **Achievements**: One-time rewards (100-50,000 soft currency)
- **Loyalty Bonuses**: Daily login rewards (100-1,000 soft currency)
- **Tournament Prizes**: 1,000-1,000,000+ soft currency

**Secondary Sources**:
- **AI Earnings**: Your AI earns while you're offline
- **Charm Bonuses**: Earning multipliers from equipped charms
- **Level-Up Rewards**: Bonus soft currency on leveling
- **Selling Assets**: Sell unwanted characters/charms
- **Clan Bonuses**: Additional earnings from clan membership
- **Referral Rewards**: Invite friends for bonuses

**Soft Currency Properties**:
- **Earnable**: Never requires real money
- **Non-Speculative**: Fixed value, no market trading
- **Account-Bound**: Cannot transfer between players
- **Abundant**: Designed so active players always have enough
- **Fair**: Earnings balanced to prevent excessive grinding

### Purchasing Soft Currency

While earnable through gameplay, players can optionally purchase soft currency:

**Purchase Options**:
- **Starter Pack**: 10,000 soft currency ($4.99)
- **Standard Pack**: 25,000 soft currency ($9.99)
- **Premium Pack**: 60,000 soft currency ($19.99)
- **Mega Pack**: 150,000 soft currency ($49.99)
- **Ultimate Pack**: 350,000 soft currency ($99.99)

**Purchase Incentives**:
- **First-Time Bonus**: 100% extra on first purchase
- **Seasonal Promotions**: Periodic bonus offers
- **Bundle Deals**: Combined with other items
- **Loyalty Bonuses**: Better rates for higher-tier members

**Payment Methods**:
- Credit/debit cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
- Regional options (Alipay, WeChat Pay, etc.)
- Token conversion (optional)

### Soft Currency Economy Design

**Anti-Inflation Measures**:
- **Fixed Exchange Rates**: Stable pricing for all items
- **Sink Mechanisms**: Multiple ways to spend currency
- **Controlled Generation**: Balanced earning rates
- **No Player Trading**: Prevents market manipulation
- **Regular Audits**: Economic monitoring and adjustment

**Player-Friendly Design**:
- New players get starter soft currency
- Generous earning rates for active play
- No paywalls for essential features
- Optional purchases are convenience, not necessity
- Free players can access all gameplay content

<Callout type="warning">
**No Pay-to-Win**: Soft currency does not provide competitive advantages. Success in Mahjong Stars depends on skill, strategy, and AI training—not spending money.
</Callout>

## Hard Currency / Token (Governance and Marketplace)

Alongside soft currency, Mahjong Stars utilizes a blockchain-based token that performs several roles for players who choose to participate in the Web3 ecosystem.

### Core Functions

**Governance**:
- **Proposal Voting**: Vote on platform features and changes
- **Parameter Adjustment**: Influence AI model settings, marketplace fees
- **Feature Prioritization**: Vote on development roadmap
- **Treasury Allocation**: Approve spending for marketing, partnerships
- **Community Initiatives**: Support charitable causes and events

**Marketplace Transactions**:
- **Asset Trading**: Buy and sell characters, charms, studios as NFTs
- **Peer-to-Peer**: Direct player-to-player transactions
- **Auction Participation**: Bid on rare items
- **AI Marketplace**: Trade trained AI models and templates
- **Rental Fees**: Pay to rent other players' assets

**Staking**:
- **Earn Rewards**: Stake tokens for passive income
- **Access Features**: Unlock premium capabilities
- **Network Security**: Support blockchain operations (depending on chain)
- **Governance Weight**: Staked tokens provide voting power
- **Tier Benefits**: Different staking levels unlock different benefits

**AI Upgrades**:
- **Advanced Modules**: Premium AI training capabilities
- **Premium Analytics**: Detailed performance tools
- **Priority Training**: Faster AI learning
- **Specialized Templates**: Purchase expert AI strategies
- **Cross-Variant AI**: Transfer learning between variants

<Callout type="info">
**Separate Entity**: The governance token is issued and managed by a separate company, allowing Mahjong Stars to operate the game independently of regulatory constraints related to token issuance.
</Callout>

### Token Acquisition

**Primary Markets**:
- **Token Launch**: Initial distribution event
- **Exchange Listings**: Purchase on cryptocurrency exchanges
- **Liquidity Pools**: Decentralized exchange (DEX) trading

**In-Platform Earning**:
- **Tournament Prizes**: High-level competitions reward tokens
- **Staking Rewards**: Earn through participation
- **Governance Rewards**: Active voters receive bonuses
- **Achievement Bonuses**: Rare accomplishments grant tokens
- **Marketplace Trading**: Profit from asset trading

**Special Events**:
- **Airdrops**: Rewards for loyal users and community members
- **Community Competitions**: Special challenges with token prizes
- **Partnership Events**: Collaborations with other projects
- **Season Rewards**: End-of-season token distributions

### Token Utility vs. Soft Currency

| Feature | Soft Currency | Token |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| **Earn Through Gameplay** | ✅ Primary method | ⚠️ Limited, high-level only |
| **Purchase Assets** | ✅ Primary store | ⚠️ Marketplace only |
| **Tournament Entry** | ✅ Most tournaments | ⚠️ Premium tournaments |
| **Governance Voting** | ❌ Not available | ✅ Primary use |
| **Staking Rewards** | ❌ Not available | ✅ Available |
| **Marketplace Trading** | ❌ Not tradeable | ✅ Primary currency |
| **Cross-Player Transfer** | ❌ Account-bound | ✅ Fully transferable |
| **Investment Potential** | ❌ Fixed value | ✅ Market-determined |

### Optional Nature of Token

**Key Principle**: The token is entirely optional for enjoying Mahjong Stars.

**Full Experience Without Token**:
- Complete gameplay access
- All Mahjong variants
- AI training and companions
- Social features
- Tournaments (most)
- Character and charm collection (via soft currency)
- Progression systems

**Token-Exclusive Features**:
- Governance participation
- Marketplace trading (NFT version of assets)
- High-stakes premium tournaments
- Advanced staking benefits
- AI model marketplace
- Cross-platform asset potential

<Callout type="success">
**Player Choice**: Mahjong Stars respects that many players prefer traditional gaming without blockchain. The token layer is purely optional enhancement.
</Callout>

## Economic Balance

### Dual System Benefits

**For Platform**:
- **Regulatory Clarity**: Soft currency avoids securities concerns
- **Flexibility**: Adjust economy without affecting token
- **Accessibility**: Attract non-crypto audience
- **Stability**: Soft currency provides economic foundation
- **Innovation**: Token enables cutting-edge features

**For Players**:
- **Choice**: Use what you're comfortable with
- **No Pressure**: Never forced into crypto
- **Full Access**: Soft currency gives complete experience
- **Upside**: Optional token participation for extras
- **Safety**: Soft currency has no market volatility

### Conversion Mechanisms

**One-Way Conversion** (Token → Soft Currency):
- Tokens can be converted to soft currency at market rates
- Conversion is one-directional to prevent economic gaming
- Provides utility for token holders
- Ensures soft currency availability
- Supports token value

**No Soft Currency → Token Conversion**:
- Prevents token inflation
- Maintains token scarcity
- Protects token economics
- Separates the two economies
- Ensures fair distribution

### Economic Monitoring

**Platform Oversight**:
- Real-time economic dashboards
- Inflation/deflation tracking
- Earning rate analysis
- Spending pattern monitoring
- Pricing optimization

**Adjustment Mechanisms**:
- Soft currency earning rate adjustments
- Item pricing updates
- Event reward tuning
- Sink creation (new spending options)
- Supply management

---

## Economic Philosophy

### Sustainable Design

**Core Principles**:
1. **Players First**: Economy serves player experience
2. **Fairness**: Equal opportunities for all
3. **Transparency**: Clear, understandable systems
4. **Flexibility**: Adapt to changing conditions
5. **Longevity**: Built for multi-year operation

### Anti-Exploitation

**Protections**:
- No gambling mechanics
- No loot boxes (only transparent raffles with published odds)
- No artificial scarcity of soft currency
- No pay-to-win advantages
- No predatory monetization

<Callout type="warning">
**Ethical Economics**: Mahjong Stars commits to ethical, player-friendly economic design that prioritizes long-term engagement over short-term extraction.
</Callout>
