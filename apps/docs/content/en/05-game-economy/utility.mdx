# Utility & Acquisition

The governance token has multiple utility functions beyond basic payment, creating a sustainable token economy with real value drivers.

---

## Utility and Use Cases

The governance token serves various purposes within the Mahjong Stars ecosystem:

### VIP Features

Certain premium features may require holding or staking a minimum number of tokens.

**Token-Gated Features**:
- **Priority Matchmaking**: Reduced wait times
- **Early Character Access**: First access to new releases
- **Enhanced Charm Drops**: Increased drop rates (10-25%)
- **Premium Analytics**: Advanced statistical tools
- **Custom Tables**: Additional customization options
- **Storage Expansion**: More replay storage, character slots

**Holding Tiers**:
- **Bronze Holder** (1,000 tokens): Basic VIP features
- **Silver Holder** (5,000 tokens): Enhanced benefits
- **Gold Holder** (10,000 tokens): Premium access
- **Platinum Holder** (25,000 tokens): Elite status
- **Diamond Holder** (50,000+ tokens): Maximum benefits

<Callout type="info">
**Hold to Unlock**: Simply holding tokens in your wallet unlocks benefits. No staking required for basic VIP features (though staking provides additional benefits).
</Callout>

### Exclusive Tournaments

High-stakes tournaments with large prize pools may require token entry fees.

**Token Tournament Types**:

**Weekly High Roller**:
- Entry: 100-500 tokens
- Prize Pool: 50,000-250,000 tokens
- Format: Swiss system, 64-256 players
- Rewards: Top 10 placements

**Monthly Championship**:
- Entry: 1,000 tokens
- Prize Pool: 500,000+ tokens + rare assets
- Format: Multi-day qualification + finals
- Rewards: Top 20 placements + special recognition

**Seasonal Grand Prix**:
- Entry: Qualification-based (no direct token fee)
- Prize Pool: 2,000,000+ tokens + legendary items
- Format: Regional qualifiers → Global finals
- Rewards: Top 50 + participation bonuses

**Benefits**:
- Higher skill competition
- Larger prize pools
- Professional recognition
- Path to esports careers
- Exclusive titles and cosmetics

### Discounts and Rebates

Staking tokens can reduce marketplace commission fees or provide soft currency rebates on purchases.

**Staking Benefits**:

| Staking Tier | Marketplace Fee Discount | Soft Currency Rebate |
|-------------|-------------------------|---------------------|
| **No Stake** | 0% (10% base fee) | 0% |
| **Bronze** (1K staked) | 10% discount (9% fee) | 5% rebate on purchases |
| **Silver** (5K staked) | 25% discount (7.5% fee) | 10% rebate |
| **Gold** (10K staked) | 40% discount (6% fee) | 15% rebate |
| **Platinum** (25K staked) | 50% discount (5% fee) | 20% rebate |
| **Diamond** (50K+ staked) | 60% discount (4% fee) | 25% rebate |

**Rebate Mechanism**:
- Automatic soft currency back on purchases
- Applied at time of transaction
- Stackable with other bonuses
- No caps on total rebates
- Encourages platform engagement

<Callout type="success">
**Real Savings**: For active traders and players, fee discounts and rebates can provide significant value over time.
</Callout>

### AI Marketplace

Players may develop and sell AI models on an AI marketplace.

**AI Marketplace Functions**:

**Selling AI Models**:
- **Trained AI Templates**: Sell your successful AI strategies
- **Specialized Modules**: Create variant-specific AI packages
- **Training Data Sets**: Sell anonymized training data
- **Strategy Guides**: AI-backed strategy documentation
- **Consulting Services**: One-on-one AI training assistance

**Pricing**:
- Sellers set own prices (in tokens)
- Platform takes 5-15% commission (reduced with staking)
- Buyers get immediate access
- Ratings and reviews system
- Popular AIs can generate significant income

**AI as Intellectual Property**:
- Protect your AI strategies
- Monetize training investment
- Build reputation as AI trainer
- Create passive income streams
- Contribute to platform ecosystem

**Buying AI Models**:
- Purchase proven strategies
- Skip training time
- Learn from experts
- Experiment with different approaches
- Accelerate competitive performance

## Circulation and Supply Management

### Controlled Supply

**Fixed Maximum Supply**:
- Total supply capped (prevents inflation)
- No additional minting (after initial distribution)
- Transparent allocation
- Predictable release schedule

**Vesting Schedules**:
- Team tokens: 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
- Advisor tokens: 2-year vesting
- Strategic partners: Custom vesting
- Early investors: 1-2 year vesting
- Community rewards: Released gradually over 5 years

**Release Schedule**:
- Prevents sudden supply shocks
- Aligns incentives long-term
- Maintains price stability
- Builds trust with holders

### Burn Mechanisms

To create deflationary pressure and maintain token value:

**Transaction Fees**:
- 1-2% of marketplace fees burned
- Permanent removal from circulation
- Automated burn process
- Transparent tracking

**Asset Purchases**:
- Portion of primary asset sales burned
- Reduces supply while driving utility
- Benefits all token holders
- Regular burn reports

**Tournament Fees**:
- Small percentage of entry fees burned
- Increases token scarcity over time
- Sustainable deflationary model

**Burn Transparency**:
- Public burn wallet address
- Regular burn announcements
- Community tracking tools
- Quarterly burn reports

### Liquidity Management

**Exchange Listings**:
- Major centralized exchanges (CEX)
- Decentralized exchanges (DEX)
- Liquidity pairs (ETH, USDT, etc.)
- Deep liquidity for minimal slippage

**Liquidity Incentives**:
- Rewards for liquidity providers
- Reduced volatility through depth
- Trading fee sharing
- Long-term LP bonuses

**Market Making**:
- Professional market makers
- Consistent bid/ask spreads
- Volume maintenance
- Price discovery support
