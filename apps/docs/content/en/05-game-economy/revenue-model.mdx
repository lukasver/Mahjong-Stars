# Revenue Model

Mahjong Stars generates revenue through multiple channels, ensuring sustainability and diversification. The revenue model prioritizes player experience while building a profitable, long-term business.

<Callout type="success">
**Sustainable Model**: Diversified revenue streams protect against market fluctuations and ensure long-term platform viability.
</Callout>

---

## Revenue Streams

### Transaction Fees

A primary revenue source from marketplace and platform activity.

**Marketplace Commissions**:
- **Digital Asset Sales**: 10% base fee (reduced for stakers)
- **AI Marketplace**: 10-15% commission
- **AI Rentals**: 5-10% commission
- **Total Volume Target**: $10M-$50M Year 1, $50M-$200M Year 3

**Fee Distribution**:
- 40% burned (token deflation)
- 30% to stakers (incentivize holding)
- 20% to development fund
- 10% to liquidity providers

**Expected Revenue**: $1M-$5M Year 1, $5M-$20M Year 3

<Callout type="info">
**Win-Win**: Fees are low enough to encourage trading while generating sustainable platform revenue.
</Callout>

### Tournament Entries

Paid tournaments generate both prize pools and platform revenue.

**Tournament Economics**:
- **Entry Fees**: Range from 100 soft currency to 5,000 tokens
- **Platform Take**: 10-20% of entry fees
- **Prize Distribution**: 80-90% to winners
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly, seasonal events

**Tournament Types**:

**Soft Currency Tournaments** (Most Popular):
- Entry: 100-10,000 soft currency
- Platform revenue: 10-20% of entries
- Expected: $500K-$2M Year 1, $2M-$10M Year 3

**Token Tournaments** (High Stakes):
- Entry: 100-5,000 tokens
- Platform revenue: 10-15% of entries
- Expected: $250K-$1M Year 1, $1M-$5M Year 3

**Tournament Revenue Drivers**:
- Large player base participation
- Compelling prize pools
- Professional production value
- Esports sponsorships
- Streaming viewership

**Expected Revenue**: $750K-$3M Year 1, $3M-$15M Year 3

### Sales of Digital Assets

Primary sale of characters, charms, studios and cosmetics.

**Primary Market Sales**:
- **Characters**: 1,000-50,000 soft currency each
- **Charms**: 500-20,000 soft currency each
- **Studios**: 50,000-500,000 tokens (auction)
- **Cosmetics**: 500-10,000 soft currency each

**Sales Strategy**:
- **Regular Releases**: New characters monthly
- **Limited Editions**: Quarterly special releases
- **Seasonal Content**: Holiday and event themes
- **Collaborations**: Brand partnerships and crossovers

**Pricing Tiers**:
- **Common Items**: $1-$5 USD equivalent
- **Uncommon Items**: $5-$15
- **Rare Items**: $15-$50
- **Epic Items**: $50-$200
- **Legendary Items**: $200-$1,000+

**Expected Volume**:
- Year 1: 500K-1M active players, $10-$30 average spending
- Year 3: 5M-10M active players, $15-$40 average spending

**Expected Revenue**: $5M-$30M Year 1, $75M-$400M Year 3

<Callout type="warning">
**Ethical Monetization**: All purchases are optional. Free players can earn everything through gameplay, just more slowly.
</Callout>

### Staking Penalties

Early unstaking fees contribute to platform revenue and token scarcity.

**Penalty Structure**:
- < 25% time elapsed: 20% penalty
- 25-50% elapsed: 15% penalty
- 50-75% elapsed: 10% penalty
- \> 75% elapsed: 5% penalty

**Penalty Distribution**:
- 50% burned (increases scarcity)
- 30% redistributed to remaining stakers
- 20% to platform treasury

**Expected Volume**:
- Assuming 10-20% annual unstaking rate
- Average 10-15% penalty
- Expected: $100K-$500K Year 1, $500K-$2M Year 3

### Advertising and Partnerships

Strategic partnerships and non-intrusive advertising.

**Advertising Approach**:
- **No Intrusive Ads**: Never interrupt gameplay
- **Branded Content**: Optional viewing for rewards
- **Sponsorships**: Tournament and event sponsorships
- **Native Integration**: Tasteful brand collaborations

**Partnership Revenue**:
- **Platform Sponsors**: Annual partnerships ($100K-$1M each)
- **Tournament Sponsors**: Event-specific deals ($10K-$500K each)
- **Content Collaborations**: Limited edition items (revenue share)
- **Cross-Promotions**: User acquisition partnerships

**Expected Revenue**: $500K-$2M Year 1, $2M-$10M Year 3

### Esports and Media Rights

Broadcast rights for major tournaments and content.

**Media Opportunities**:
- **Streaming Rights**: Platform for broadcasting tournaments
- **Sponsorship Deals**: Team and player sponsorships
- **Media Partnerships**: Traditional and digital media
- **Content Licensing**: Clips, highlights, documentaries

**Esports Revenue**:
- **Advertising**: In-stream advertising during broadcasts
- **Tickets**: Virtual and physical event tickets
- **Merchandise**: Team and player merchandise
- **Franchise Fees**: Professional team entry (long-term)

**Development Phases**:
- Year 1: Foundation, amateur tournaments
- Year 2: Semi-pro leagues, sponsorships
- Year 3+: Professional leagues, major media deals

**Expected Revenue**: $100K-$500K Year 1, $1M-$5M Year 3, $10M+ Year 5

## Cost Structure

### Operating Expenses

**Development & Engineering**:
- Team salaries: $3M-$10M annually (Year 1-3)
- Infrastructure: $500K-$3M annually
- Third-party services: $250K-$1M annually

**Marketing & User Acquisition**:
- Digital marketing: $2M-$10M annually
- Influencer partnerships: $500K-$3M annually
- Community management: $300K-$1M annually
- Events and tournaments: $500K-$5M annually

**Operations**:
- Customer support: $500K-$2M annually
- Legal and compliance: $300K-$1M annually
- Administration: $200K-$500K annually

**Total Operating Costs**: $7.55M-$35.5M Year 1, $15M-$70M Year 3

### Profitability Targets

**Break-Even Analysis**:
- Month 18-24 projected break-even
- Operating margin target: 30-50% by Year 3
- Reinvestment in growth: 40-60% of profits

## Financial Sustainability

### Diversification Strategy

**Multiple Revenue Streams**:
- No single source exceeds 60% of revenue
- Geographic diversification
- Product line expansion
- Defensive and offensive revenue mix

### Scenario Planning

**Worst Case** (Market Downturn):
- Asset sales decline 50%
- Tournament participation down 30%
- Marketplace volume reduced 40%
- Contingency: Cut costs 30%, survive 12-18 months on reserves

**Base Case** (Moderate Growth):
- As projected in moderate columns
- Healthy profitability by Year 2
- Self-sustaining by Month 18

**Best Case** (Rapid Growth):
- Exceed optimistic projections
- Accelerate development roadmap
- Geographic expansion ahead of schedule
- Multiple game platform early
