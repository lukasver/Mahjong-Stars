## Trading and Liquidity

The marketplace provides a secure, transparent platform for asset trading.

### Listing Tools

Sellers have flexible options for listing their assets:

**Fixed Price Listings**:
- Set your price in tokens
- Instant sale when buyer accepts
- No time limit (until canceled or sold)
- Best for common items with established prices
- Lowest risk, predictable outcome

**Auction Listings**:
- **English Auction (Ascending)**:
  - Set starting bid and reserve price
  - Buyers bid against each other
  - Highest bid wins at auction end
  - Typically 24-72 hour duration
  - Best for rare, high-value items

- **Dutch Auction (Descending)**:
  - Start at high price, decreases over time
  - First buyer to accept current price wins
  - Price drops on schedule (e.g., every hour)
  - Creates urgency for buyers
  - Good for moving inventory quickly

**Bundle Listings**:
- Sell multiple items together
- Offer package deals
- Better value for buyers
- Quick sale of related items
- Custom bundle pricing

**Listing Fees**:
- Small flat fee to list (0.5-1% of value, max 100 tokens)
- Refunded if item doesn't sell (option)
- Prevents spam listings
- Reduced fees for stakers

<Callout type="success">
**Low Barriers**: Minimal listing fees ensure anyone can participate in the marketplace economy.
</Callout>

### Search and Filters

Buyers can easily find what they're looking for:

**Search Features**:
- **Text Search**: Search by name, description, traits
- **Advanced Filters**:
  - Asset type (Character, Charm, Studio, AI Module)
  - Rarity tier (Common to Legendary)
  - Level range
  - Price range
  - Seller rating
  - Listing type (auction vs. fixed)
  - Date listed (newest first, etc.)
- **Sorting Options**:
  - Price (low to high, high to low)
  - Popularity (most viewed)
  - Ending soon (auctions)
  - Recently listed
  - Best deals (value rating)

**Smart Filters**:
- AI-recommended items based on your collection
- Similar items (when viewing a listing)
- Price history and trends
- Comparative analysis tools
- Watched items alerts

**Trending Section**:
- Most traded items this week
- Rising in value assets
- Popular searches
- Community favorites
- Seasonal highlights

### Secure Escrow

All transactions are processed via smart contracts, ensuring safety:

**Transaction Security**:
1. Buyer sends payment to escrow contract
2. Seller transfers asset to escrow
3. Smart contract verifies both sides
4. Simultaneous exchange executed
5. Both parties receive their items atomically

**Protection Features**:
- **No Scams**: Can't take money without giving item
- **No Chargebacks**: Blockchain transactions are final
- **Dispute Resolution**: Platform mediation for issues
- **Rating System**: Build seller/buyer reputation
- **Buyer Protection**: Verify assets before finalizing

**Smart Contract Benefits**:
- Trustless transactions
- Transparent code
- Audited security
- Minimal fees (gas only)
- Instant settlement

### Analytics Dashboard

Data-driven trading decisions:

**Historical Prices**:
- Price charts for every item
- Volume tracking
- Average sale prices
- Floor prices (lowest available)
- Ceiling prices (highest sold)

**Market Trends**:
- Supply and demand indicators
- Price momentum
- Trading velocity
- Holder distribution
- Rarity multipliers

**Portfolio Tracking**:
- Your collection current value
- Purchase prices vs. current value
- Profit/loss calculations
- Best performers
- Investment analytics

**Alerts**:
- Price drop notifications
- Listing alerts for wanted items
- Bid outbid notifications
- Auction ending reminders
- Portfolio value changes

<Callout type="warning">
**Market Intelligence**: Use analytics to make informed trading decisions, not speculative gambles. Focus on items you'll use and enjoy.
</Callout>

## AI-as-a-Service and Monetization Potential

A revolutionary feature unique to Mahjong Stars: AI marketplace.

### AI Rentals

Players with well-trained AI companions can rent them to others:

**Rental Mechanics**:
- **Hourly Rentals**: 10-100 tokens/hour (set by owner)
- **Daily Rentals**: 100-1,000 tokens/day
- **Match-Based**: 50-500 tokens per match
- **Subscription**: Weekly/monthly rental packages

**Rental Features**:
- Renter uses AI in matches
- Earnings go to renter (AI owner gets rental fee)
- AI performance tracked
- Rating system for AI quality
- Cancellation policies

**Owner Benefits**:
- Passive income from training investment
- Platform takes 5-10% commission
- Build reputation as AI trainer
- Higher demand = higher prices
- Multiple simultaneous rentals (if allowed)

**Performance Guarantees**:
- Advertised win rates must be accurate
- Refunds for underperforming AIs
- Verification system
- Community reviews
- Dispute resolution

<Callout type="success">
**Innovative Economy**: Well-trained AIs become valuable assets generating continuous income for their trainers.
</Callout>

### AI Coaching Services

Skilled players can offer one-on-one coaching using their AI models:

**Coaching Options**:
- **Strategy Sessions**: Analyze replays with AI insights (500-5,000 tokens/hour)

- **Live Coaching**: Watch and advise during games (1,000-10,000 tokens/session)
- **AI Optimization**: Tune AI settings for performance (500-5,000 tokens)

**Booking System**:
- Schedule through marketplace
- Escrow protects both parties
- Rating and review system
- Cancellation policies
- Session recordings (with permission)

**Coach Benefits**:
- Monetize expertise
- Build personal brand
- Grow coaching business
- Platform promotion (top coaches featured)
- Community recognition

### AI Templates and Modules

Developers may create AI templates specializing in particular strategies:

**AI Products**:
- **Variant Specialists**: AIs optimized for specific rule sets (1,000-10,000 tokens)
- **Strategy Modules**: Defensive, aggressive, balanced styles (500-5,000 tokens)

## Physical Collectibles Exchange

Unique to Mahjong Stars: trade physical items linked to the digital economy.

### Physical Collectibles

Occasionally released limited-edition physical merchandise:

**Item Types**:
- **Themed Mugs**: Character designs, limited runs
- **Art Prints**: Platform artwork, signed editions
- **Apparel**: T-shirts, hoodies with exclusive designs
- **Mahjong Sets**: Physical tile sets with special designs
- **Commemorative Items**: Event memorabilia

**Acquisition**:
- Purchase with soft currency from in-game shop
- Limited quantities
- First-come, first-served
- May require minimum account level
- Shipped to verified address

<Callout type="info">
**Digital-Physical Bridge**: Physical collectibles create tangible connection to the digital platform, enhancing community engagement.
</Callout>

### Exchange Mechanics

Players can relist physical items they've acquired:

**Listing Process**:
1. Own physical collectible (shipped to you)
2. List on dedicated physical exchange
3. Set your asking price (soft currency or tokens)
4. Other users place bids
5. Auction ends, highest bidder wins
6. Winner pays, you ship item
7. Delivery confirmation releases payment

**Auction Format**:
- Minimum bid requirements
- Auction duration (7-14 days typical)
- Reserve price optional
- Buy-it-now option
- Shipping cost transparency

**Safety Features**:
- Verified addresses
- Tracking required
- Escrow until delivery confirmed
- Dispute resolution
- Seller ratings crucial

**Platform Role**:
- Host listings (no commission on physical)
- Mediate disputes
- Verify authenticity
- Provide guidelines
- Community policing

## Marketplace Fees

To maintain platform and provide value:

**Transaction Fees**:
- **Digital Assets**: 10% base commission
- **Staker Discounts**: 10-60% fee reduction (see Staking tiers)
- **AI Rentals**: 5-10% commission
- **AI Products**: 10-15% commission
- **Physical Items**: 0% commission (community-driven)

**Fee Distribution**:
- 40% burned (deflationary)
- 30% to stakers (rewards)
- 20% to development fund
- 10% to liquidity providers

**Gas Fees**:
- Blockchain transaction costs (paid by user)
- Typically small (pennies to dollars)
- Dynamic based on network congestion
- Subsidy programs for new users (first transactions free)
