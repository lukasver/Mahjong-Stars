# Staking & Rewards

Staking allows token holders to lock up their assets in exchange for rewards and privileges. Mahjong Stars offers multiple staking tiers with progressively better benefits.

---

## Staking Mechanism and Tiers

Mahjong Stars offers a tiered staking system where higher stakes unlock better benefits and returns.

### Staking Tiers

<Callout type="success">
**Flexible Staking**: Choose duration and tier based on your goals. Longer staking periods provide better rewards but require patience.
</Callout>

#### Bronze Tier

**Requirements**:
- Minimum Stake: 1,000 tokens
- Minimum Duration: 30 days

**Benefits**:
- **Soft Currency Rebates**: 5% on all purchases
- **Marketplace Fee Reduction**: 10% discount (9% fee instead of 10%)
- **Weekly Raffle Tickets**: 1 automatic entry
- **Staking APY**: 5-8% annual return
- **Basic Governance**: Full voting rights

**Ideal For**: Entry-level participants, casual players exploring staking

#### Silver Tier

**Requirements**:
- Minimum Stake: 5,000 tokens
- Minimum Duration: 90 days

**Benefits**:
- **Soft Currency Rebates**: 10% on all purchases
- **Marketplace Fee Reduction**: 25% discount (7.5% fee)
- **Weekly Raffle Tickets**: 2 automatic entries
- **Tournament Entry Discounts**: 10% off
- **Staking APY**: 8-12% annual return
- **Priority Support**: Faster customer service
- **Exclusive Tournaments**: Access to Silver+ events
- **Enhanced Governance**: Proposal creation rights

**Ideal For**: Active players, moderate investors

#### Gold Tier

**Requirements**:
- Minimum Stake: 10,000 tokens
- Minimum Duration: 180 days

**Benefits**:
- **Soft Currency Rebates**: 15% on all purchases
- **Marketplace Fee Reduction**: 40% discount (6% fee)
- **Weekly Raffle Tickets**: 3 automatic entries
- **Tournament Entry Discounts**: 15% off
- **Early Character Access**: First access to new releases
- **Staking APY**: 12-18% annual return
- **Priority Matchmaking**: Reduced wait times
- **AI Training Queue**: Priority in AI training
- **Premium Analytics**: Advanced statistical tools
- **Gold-Exclusive Tournaments**: Special competitions
- **Enhanced Governance**: Weighted voting power (1.5x)

**Ideal For**: Serious players, significant investors

#### Platinum Tier

**Requirements**:
- Minimum Stake: 25,000 tokens
- Minimum Duration: 270 days

**Benefits**:
- **Soft Currency Rebates**: 20% on all purchases
- **Marketplace Fee Reduction**: 50% discount (5% fee)
- **Weekly Raffle Tickets**: 5 automatic entries
- **Tournament Entry Discounts**: 20% off
- **Rare Charm Airdrops**: Occasional exclusive items
- **Staking APY**: 18-25% annual return

- **Beta Testing**: Early feature access

#### Diamond Tier

**Requirements**:

<Callout type="info">
**Progressive Benefits**: Each tier builds on the previous, so Diamond stakers get all benefits from Bronze through Platinum plus their exclusive perks.
</Callout>

### Staking Durations

Different lock-up periods provide different reward multipliers:

**Duration Options**:
- **30 Days**: Base APY rate
- **90 Days**: +20% APY bonus
- **180 Days**: +40% APY bonus
- **270 Days**: +60% APY bonus
- **365 Days**: +100% APY bonus (double rewards)

**Flexibility**:
- Choose any duration at or above tier minimum
- Auto-renew option at expiration
- Compound rewards automatically
- Partial unstaking available (with penalties)

### Staking Mechanics

**How to Stake**:
1. Connect wallet to platform
2. Navigate to staking dashboard
3. Choose tier and duration
4. Confirm transaction
5. Receive staking NFT (represents position)
6. Start earning immediately

**Reward Calculation**:
- Rewards calculated per block
- Distributed weekly or compound automatically
- Based on: stake amount × APY × duration multiplier × tier bonus
- No maximum cap on rewards

**Early Unstaking**:
- Allowed but incurs penalties
- **< 25% time elapsed**: 20% penalty
- **25-50% time elapsed**: 15% penalty
- **50-75% time elapsed**: 10% penalty
- **> 75% time elapsed**: 5% penalty
- Penalties burned (benefits all holders)

## Governance Participation and Voting Rights

Active participants can propose changes to the ecosystem and vote on important decisions.

### Governance Portal

**Access**:
- All token holders can vote
- Staking increases voting weight
- No minimum holding requirement for voting
- Proposal creation requires Gold tier+ or 10,000 tokens

**Proposal Categories**:

**Game Features**:
- New Mahjong variants
- Gameplay mechanics adjustments
- AI model parameter changes
- Tournament format modifications
- Feature prioritization

**Economic Adjustments**:
- Soft currency earning rates
- Marketplace fee structures
- Staking rewards adjustment
- Token utility expansion
- Fee distribution changes

**Allocation Decisions**:
- Marketing budget approval
- Partnership investments
- Development priorities
- Community events
- Charitable initiatives

**Platform Governance**:
- Rule changes
- Terms of service updates
- Community guidelines
- Moderation policies
- Platform partnerships

### Voting Process

**Proposal Submission**:
1. Create detailed proposal
2. Community discussion period (7 days)
3. Revision based on feedback
4. Official submission
5. Voting period (7-14 days)
6. Implementation if passed

**Voting Power**:
- **1 Token = 1 Vote** (base)
- **Staking Multipliers**:
  - Bronze/Silver: 1x
  - Gold: 1.5x
  - Platinum: 2x
  - Diamond: 3x
- **Long-term Holder Bonus**: +10-50% based on hold time

**Passing Requirements**:
- **Minor Changes**: 51% approval, 5% quorum
- **Major Changes**: 66% approval, 10% quorum
- **Critical Changes**: 75% approval, 20% quorum
- **Constitution Changes**: 90% approval, 30% quorum

### Voter Incentives

To encourage participation, voters receive rewards:

**Participation Rewards**:
- **Vote on Proposal**: 10-50 tokens (based on proposal importance)
- **Winning Side Bonus**: Additional 50% if your vote was with majority
- **Consistent Voter**: Monthly bonuses for regular participation
- **Quality Proposals**: Tokens for creating passed proposals (1,000-10,000)

**Delegation**:
- Delegate voting power to trusted representatives
- Maintain voting rewards
- Revoke delegation anytime
- Partial delegation possible

<Callout type="success">
**Democratic Platform**: Governance ensures Mahjong Stars evolves according to community wishes, not just team decisions.
</Callout>

## Reward Distribution

### Reward Sources

**Where Rewards Come From**:
- **In-Game Rewards Allocation**: 33.33% of total supply reserved
- **Marketplace Fees**: Portion redistributed to stakers
- **Tournament Entry Fees**: Percentage added to reward pools
- **Platform Revenue**: Share allocated to token holders
- **Partnership Income**: Strategic collaborations contribute

### Distribution Schedule

**Gradual Release**:
- In-game rewards released over 5-7 years
- Prevents inflation and maintains scarcity
- Adjusts based on platform growth
- Emergency reserve for market conditions

**Dynamic Adjustment**:
- Rewards scale with platform activity
- More users = more rewards available
- Sustainable long-term model
- Prevents early depletion

## Staking Best Practices

### Optimization Strategies

**For Maximum Returns**:
- Stake highest amount you're comfortable with
- Choose longest duration you can commit to
- Compound rewards automatically
- Participate in governance (additional rewards)
- Combine with marketplace activity (fee savings)

**Risk Management**:
- Don't stake more than you can afford to lock
- Diversify across tiers and durations
- Keep liquid tokens for trading
- Understand unstaking penalties
- Monitor market conditions

**Tax Considerations**:
- Staking rewards may be taxable
- Consult local tax professional
- Track all reward distributions
- Document unstaking events
- Maintain accurate records

<Callout type="info">
**Long-Term Thinking**: Staking is designed for believers in the platform's long-term success. Short-term traders may prefer keeping tokens liquid.
</Callout>
