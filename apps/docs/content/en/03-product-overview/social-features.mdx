# Social Features

Mahjong is fundamentally a social game. Mahjong Stars builds upon this by providing a comprehensive suite of social features to encourage interaction, collaboration and community growth.

<Callout type="success">
**Community First**: Every social feature is designed to foster genuine connections, friendly competition, and a vibrant global community.
</Callout>

---

## Chat Functionality and Friend Lists

Communication lies at the heart of any successful online community. Mahjong Stars implements robust communication tools that enhance the social experience.

### Integrated Chat System

An in-game chat system allows players to communicate before, during and after matches.

**Chat Types**:

#### General Channels
- **Global Chat**: Platform-wide discussion
- **Regional Channels**: Language and region-specific
- **Game Variant Channels**: Riichi, Bang Bang, etc.
- **Help Channel**: Get assistance from community
- **Trading Channel**: Asset marketplace discussions

#### Match-Specific Chats
- **Table Chat**: Communication during active games
- **Quick Chat Options**: Predefined phrases for fast communication
- **Emote System**: Express emotions and reactions
- **Post-Match Chat**: Discussion after game ends

#### Clan Channels
- **Clan Global**: All clan members
- **Squad Chat**: Specific team communications
- **Officer Chat**: Clan leadership
- **Announcements**: Important clan updates

#### Private Messages
- **Direct Messages**: One-on-one conversations
- **Group Messages**: Small group discussions
- **Message History**: Persistent conversation storage
- **Notification System**: Alerts for new messages

<Callout type="info">
**Moderation**: All chat channels have moderation tools to ensure respectful conduct and positive community atmosphere.
</Callout>

### Moderation and Safety

**Tools**:
- Profanity filtering (adjustable)
- Report system for inappropriate behavior
- Mute and block functions
- Moderator oversight
- Automated pattern detection
- Appeal process for penalties

**Community Guidelines**:
- Respect for all players
- No harassment or discrimination
- No spamming or advertising
- No cheating or exploit discussion
- Language-appropriate content

### Friend Lists

Players can add friends and see when they are online.

**Friend System Features**:
- **Add Friends**: Search by username or player ID
- **Online Status**: See who's available to play
- **Friend Requests**: Accept or decline incoming requests
- **Friend Categories**: Organize friends into groups
- **Favorite Friends**: Mark close friends for priority display
- **Friend Limit**: Expandable through charms or achievements

**Friend Benefits**:
- Quick invite to games
- Spectate friend matches
- Share achievements and replays
- Friend-exclusive tournaments
- Bonus rewards for playing together
- Gift system for soft currency items

### Recent Opponents

Track and reconnect with recent players:

**Features**:
- View last 50 opponents
- Quick add to friends
- Rematch requests
- Rating and review system
- Block option for problematic players

## Private Rooms and Customization Options

Private rooms allow players to create bespoke game environments tailored to their preferences.

### Room Creation and Management

**Basic Setup**:
- **Room Creation**: Simple creation process
- **Password Protection**: Secure room access
- **Player Limits**: 4 players standard, customizable for variants
- **Host Controls**: Room owner has administrative powers
- **Room Persistence**: Rooms stay open until closed or inactive

**Room Settings**:
- Choose Mahjong variant (Riichi, Bang Bang, etc.)
- Set house rules and modifications
- Configure time limits and round counts
- Define stakes (if applicable)
- Enable/disable spectators
- Recording and streaming options

<Callout type="warning">
**Fair Play**: Even in private rooms, anti-cheat systems remain active to maintain platform integrity.
</Callout>

### Customization Options

Room hosts can extensively customize their gaming environment:

**Visual Customization**:
- **Background Themes**: Choose from multiple options (traditional, modern, seasonal)
- **Table Designs**: Different table materials and styles
- **Tile Sets**: Various tile designs and themes
- **Lighting**: Adjust ambiance and mood
- **Animations**: Enable/disable various effects

**Audio Customization**:
- **Background Music**: Select from music library or upload (within guidelines)
- **Sound Effects**: Adjust tile sounds and game effects
- **Ambient Sounds**: Caf√©, parlor, nature sounds
- **Voice Chat**: Enable/disable, adjust volumes

**Gameplay Customization**:
- **Speed Settings**: Faster or slower turn timers
- **Hint Availability**: Allow/restrict AI hints
- **Pause Options**: Enable strategic timeouts
- **Scoring Variants**: Modify scoring rules
- **Special Rules**: Implement house rules

### Invitations and Access Control

Hosts decide who can enter their private rooms:

**Invitation Methods**:
- **Direct Invites**: Send to specific friends
- **Friend-of-Friend**: Allow friends to bring guests
- **Clan Invites**: Open to clan members
- **Public-Private**: Listed publicly but password protected
- **Share Link**: Generate shareable room link

**Access Management**:
- Whitelist specific players
- Blacklist problematic players
- Clan-only restriction
- Rank requirement minimums
- VIP/premium member only

### Streaming Integration

Room hosts can stream their matches directly to platforms:

**Streaming Features**:
- **Platform Support**: Twitch, YouTube, Facebook Gaming
- **Integrated Overlays**: Show player names, scores, character avatars
- **Delay Options**: Add broadcast delay for competitive integrity
- **Chat Integration**: Viewer chat appears in game (optional)
- **Highlight Reel**: Auto-generate interesting moments
- **VOD Storage**: Automatic replay archival

**Streamer Tools**:
- Camera angles and views
- Spectator mode for casters
- Replay controls
- Graphics and lower thirds
- Sponsor logo placement

<Callout type="success">
**Content Creation**: Streaming integration makes it easy to share gameplay with broader audiences and grow the Mahjong Stars community.
</Callout>

## Achievements, Replays and Social Media Integration

To reward continued engagement and facilitate content sharing, Mahjong Stars offers comprehensive achievement and sharing systems.

### Achievement System

Players can earn badges and titles for milestones and accomplishments.

**Achievement Categories**:

**Gameplay Achievements**:
- Win X matches (Bronze: 10, Silver: 100, Gold: 1000, Platinum: 10000)
- Achieve specific hands or combos
- Win with perfect scores
- Complete matches without hints
- Speed victories

**Progression Achievements**:
- Reach rank milestones
- Level character to max
- Train AI companions
- Complete skill trees
- Prestige characters

**Collection Achievements**:
- Acquire character sets
- Collect all charms of a tier
- Unlock rare items
- Complete narrative chapters
- Fill collection albums

**Social Achievements**:
- Make X friends
- Join/create clans
- Participate in tournaments
- Share replays
- Help new players

**Special Achievements**:
- Seasonal event completion
- Limited-time challenges
- Community milestones
- Unique circumstances (very rare)

**Achievement Rewards**:
- Soft currency bonuses
- Exclusive titles
- Special emotes
- Cosmetic items
- Profile customization options

### Replay System

Every match can be saved and rewatched with powerful replay features.

**Replay Features**:
- **Full Match Recording**: Every move captured
- **Multiple Angles**: View from any player perspective
- **Pause and Scrub**: Analyze any moment
- **Speed Control**: Fast-forward or slow-motion
- **Highlight Markers**: Auto-marked key moments
- **Statistical Overlay**: Show probabilities and tile counts
- **Commentary Mode**: Add voice or text commentary
- **Share Function**: Generate shareable links

**Analysis Tools**:
- Turn-by-turn breakdown
- AI evaluation of decisions
- Probability calculations
- Alternative play suggestions
- Comparison to optimal strategy
- Heat maps and visualizations

**Replay Storage**:
- **Recent Matches**: Last 50 matches auto-saved
- **Favorites**: Save important replays permanently
- **Tournament Matches**: All tournament games saved
- **Cloud Storage**: Access from any device
- **Download**: Export replays to local storage

<Callout type="info">
**Learning Tool**: Replays are invaluable for studying strategy, improving play, and understanding mistakes.
</Callout>

Players can share achievements and replays directly on social platforms.

**Sharing Options**:
- **Automatic Clips**: AI generates exciting highlight reels
- **Custom Clips**: Create manual highlight selections
- **Achievement Posts**: Share milestones and accomplishments
- **Screenshot Tools**: Capture and annotate moments
- **GIF Creation**: Short animated clips for sharing

**Supported Platforms**:
- Twitter/X (automated posts with stats)
- Facebook (detailed match summaries)
- Instagram (visual highlights and stories)
- Discord (direct server integration)
- TikTok (short-form video clips)
- YouTube (full match uploads)

**Sharing Features**:

- **Template Posts**: Pre-formatted social posts
- **Hashtag Integration**: Automatic relevant tags
- **Community Challenges**: Participate in trending events
- **Friend Tagging**: Tag opponents and teammates

**Benefits**:
- **Organic Marketing**: Players promote the platform
- **Community Growth**: Attract new players
- **Social Proof**: Showcase exciting gameplay
- **Content Creation**: Build personal brand
- **Engagement**: Foster discussions outside platform

### Profile and Statistics

**Public Profile**:
- **Player Card**: Customizable identity showcase
- **Statistics Dashboard**: Win rates, rankings, achievements
- **Character Gallery**: Display favorite characters
- **Achievement Showcase**: Feature earned badges
- **Match History**: Public or private, player choice
- **Activity Feed**: Recent highlights and activity

**Privacy Controls**:
- **Visibility Settings**: Public, friends-only, or private
- **Selective Sharing**: Choose what to display
- **Anonymous Mode**: Hide identity in casual matches
- **Data Protection**: GDPR-compliant privacy options
