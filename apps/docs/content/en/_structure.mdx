# Mahjong Stars Documentation Structure

## Complete Documentation Hierarchy

Welcome to the comprehensive documentation for **Mahjong Stars** - a revolutionary web3 social competitive hybrid AI-powered multiplayer Mahjong platform. This documentation structure provides all stakeholders with detailed information about our innovative gaming platform.

### Platform Overview

#### 1. Introduction ([01-introduction](/01-introduction))
- **Purpose**: Platform overview and core value proposition
- **Content**: Mahjong Stars platform overview, cultural significance of Mahjong, vision and mission, dual-layer architecture
- **Audience**: All users, investors, partners

#### 2. Market Opportunity ([02-market-opportunity](/02-market-opportunity))
- **Purpose**: Market analysis and competitive positioning
- **Content**: Global Mahjong landscape, market potential (600-700M players), competitive advantages, target audience
- **Audience**: Investors, strategic partners, market analysts

### Platform Features

#### 3. Product Overview ([03-product-overview](/03-product-overview))
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive guide to gameplay and features
- **Subsections**:
  - **Gameplay Mechanics**: Mahjong variants, play modes, tournaments, leaderboards
  - **AI Integration**: AI-assisted gameplay, personalized AI players, hybrid play features
  - **Collectibles**: Character assets, charm assets, studio assets
  - **Social Features**: Chat, friend lists, private rooms, achievements
- **Audience**: Players, developers, feature enthusiasts

#### 4. Player Experience ([04-player-experience](/04-player-experience))
- **Purpose**: User experience and accessibility guide
- **Subsections**:
  - **Accessibility**: Browser-based platform, account creation, wallet integration
  - **UI/UX Design**: Interface design, tutorials, multilingual support
  - **Progression**: Leveling systems, rewards, loyalty programs
- **Audience**: New players, UX designers, accessibility advocates

#### 5. Game Economy ([05-game-economy](/05-game-economy))
- **Purpose**: Complete economic framework and tokenomics
- **Subsections**:
  - **Dual Currency**: Soft currency and governance token mechanics
  - **Utility**: Token use cases, acquisition, circulation
  - **Staking & Rewards**: Staking tiers, gameplay rewards, governance voting
  - **Marketplace**: Trading, AI-as-a-Service, physical collectibles
  - **Revenue Model**: Transaction fees, tournament entries, sales channels
  - **Tokenomics**: Distribution allocation, vesting schedules, reward mechanics
- **Audience**: Investors, token holders, financial analysts, economists

### Compliance & Strategy

#### 6. Regulatory Compliance ([06-regulatory-compliance](/06-regulatory-compliance))
- **Purpose**: Legal framework and risk management
- **Content**: Skill-based platform positioning, digital services standards, platform integrity, jurisdictional awareness
- **Audience**: Legal teams, regulators, compliance officers, investors

#### 7. Roadmap ([07-roadmap](/07-roadmap))
- **Purpose**: Strategic timeline and development milestones
- **Content**: Phase 1 (2026 Q1-Q2), Phase 2 (2026 Q3-2027 Q1), Phase 3 (2027 Q2-2028 Q1), long-term vision
- **Audience**: Investors, team members, strategic partners

#### 8. Team ([08-team](/08-team))
- **Purpose**: Leadership team and expertise
- **Content**: Jonas Alm (Founder & CEO), Lars Broberg (COO), team credentials and achievements
- **Audience**: Investors, partners, potential team members

### Resources

#### 9. Glossary ([09-glossary](/09-glossary))
- **Purpose**: Comprehensive terminology reference
- **Content**: Mahjong terms, blockchain/Web3 terminology, AI and gaming terms
- **Audience**: All users, especially newcomers to Mahjong or Web3

#### 10. Conclusion ([10-conclusion](/10-conclusion))
