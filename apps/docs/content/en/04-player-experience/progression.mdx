# Player Progression & Retention

Progression is an essential component of long-term engagement. Mahjong Stars incorporates multiple leveling systems, reward mechanisms, and competitive structures to keep players motivated and engaged.

---

## Leveling Systems and AI Growth

Mahjong Stars features interconnected progression systems that provide continuous advancement opportunities.

### Player Level

Reflects overall experience and unlocks new features as players advance.

**Level Progression**:
- **Starting Level**: 1 (all players begin here)
- **Maximum Level**: 100 (with room for expansion)
- **Experience Sources**: Matches played, quests completed, achievements earned
- **Level Requirements**: Exponentially increasing XP needed

**Experience (XP) Earning**:
- **Match Completion**: 50-200 XP (based on performance)
- **Wins**: Bonus 100-300 XP
- **Perfect Games**: Additional 500 XP
- **Daily Quests**: 200-1,000 XP each
- **Weekly Challenges**: 1,000-5,000 XP
- **Achievements**: 500-10,000 XP
- **Tournament Participation**: 1,000-50,000 XP

**Feature Unlocks by Level**:

| Level Range | Unlocked Features |
|------------|-------------------|
| 1-10 | Basic gameplay, tutorials, casual matches |
| 11-20 | Ranked play, first AI slot, daily quests |
| 21-30 | Advanced tutorials, tournament access, clan joining |
| 31-40 | Studio access (if owned), AI rental capability, custom rooms |
| 41-50 | Advanced tournaments, clan creation, marketplace selling |
| 51-60 | High-stakes tables, premium features, mentorship program |
| 61-70 | Exclusive tournaments, early feature access |
| 71-80 | Prestige options, legendary item access |
| 81-90 | Elite competitions, platform testing program |
| 91-100 | Maximum benefits, special recognition, exclusive content |

**Level-Up Rewards**:
- Soft currency bonuses
- Charm rewards
- Character shards
- Raffle tickets
- Cosmetic unlocks
- Title options

<Callout type="success">
**Always Progressing**: Even max-level players continue earning rewards through prestige systems and seasonal resets.
</Callout>

### Character Level

Each character accrues experience through matches, unlocking additional features and benefits.

**Character Progression**:
- **Individual XP**: Each character levels separately
- **Active Character Gains XP**: Only the equipped character
- **Max Level**: 50 per character
- **Prestige Option**: Reset to level 1 with permanent bonuses

**Character XP Sources**:
- Using character in matches (primary)
- Winning with character (bonus)
- Special character challenges
- Character-specific achievements
- Event participation

**Leveling Benefits**:
- **Cosmetic Options**: Unlocked at specific levels
- **Emotes**: Character-specific expressions (levels 5, 15, 25, 35, 45)
- **AI Efficiency**: Improved AI training speed (+2% per level)
- **Stat Bonuses**: Minor attribute increases
- **Skill Points**: Allocate to skill trees (1 point per level)

**Skill Tree Allocation**:

**Strategic Branch**:
- AI training speed bonuses
- Additional AI slot unlocks (level 20, 40)
- Enhanced post-game analysis
- Strategy hint improvements
- Probability calculation aids

**Social Branch**:
- Unique emotes and stickers
- Chat customization options
- Friend bonus multipliers
- Clan contribution increases
- Social XP bonuses

**Economic Branch**:
- Soft currency earning bonuses (+1% per point)
- Raffle ticket multipliers
- Tournament entry discounts (up to 25%)
- Marketplace fee reductions
- Charm drop rate increases

**Character Prestige**:
- Available at level 50
- Reset to level 1 but keep skill points
- Earn permanent stat bonuses
- Visual prestige indicators
- Exclusive cosmetic options
- Multiple prestige levels possible

### AI Level

As AI companions play more matches, they gain levels resulting in improved performance.

**AI Progression Mechanics**:
- **Data-Based Leveling**: Improves with more gameplay data
- **Match Experience**: Every match contributes to AI growth
- **Max Level**: 50 (per AI instance)
- **Quality Tiers**: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond

**AI Level Benefits**:
- **Faster Decisions**: Reduced think time (5-50% faster)
- **Better Risk Assessment**: Improved probability calculations
- **Enhanced Adaptation**: Quicker adjustment to opponents
- **Strategy Refinement**: More sophisticated play patterns
- **Consistency**: More reliable performance

**AI Training Requirements**:
- **Initial Training**: 50-100 games for basic functionality
- **Competency**: 200-500 games for reliable performance
- **Excellence**: 1,000+ games for top-tier AI
- **Maintenance**: Continuous play keeps AI current
- **Retraining**: Update AI with new strategies

**AI Training Acceleration**:
- **Studio Training Facilities**: Faster training
- **Training Modules**: Purchasable XP boosts
- **Simulation Matches**: Count toward AI experience
- **Targeted Training**: Focus on specific weaknesses
- **Bulk Training**: Train multiple AIs simultaneously (Studio feature)

<Callout type="info">
**Living AI**: Your AI continuously evolves as you play, adapting to meta changes and refining strategies based on accumulated experience.
</Callout>

## Rewards and Loyalty Programs

To incentivise frequent play, Mahjong Stars offers comprehensive reward systems.

### Daily Quests

Simple objectives completed through normal gameplay.

**Quest Types**:
- **Win X Matches**: Win 3 matches (Reward: 1,000 soft currency)
- **Play Y Games**: Complete 5 games regardless of outcome (Reward: 500 soft currency)
- **Use Specific Character**: Play 3 games with featured character (Reward: Character XP boost)
- **Social Quests**: Play with friends, send gifts (Reward: Social items)
- **Variant Quests**: Play specific Mahjong variant (Reward: Variant-specific rewards)

**Quest System**:
- **Daily Reset**: New quests every 24 hours
- **Quest Slots**: 3-5 active quests simultaneously
- **Reroll Option**: Replace one quest per day (soft currency cost)
- **Streak Bonuses**: Consecutive days multiply rewards
- **Quick Completion**: Most quests achievable in 30-60 minutes

### Weekly Challenges

Longer-term goals requiring strategic planning.

**Challenge Examples**:
- **Win with Specific Hands**: Achieve particular winning patterns (3 times)
- **Reach Rank Milestone**: Climb ranked ladder (Reward: Rank-based prizes)
- **Tournament Performance**: Place top 50 in weekly tournament
- **Collection Goals**: Acquire 5 new charms
- **AI Training**: Play 50 games with AI (Reward: AI XP boost)

**Weekly Challenge Benefits**:
- **Larger Rewards**: Charm bundles, character shards, significant soft currency
- **Strategic Depth**: Requires planning and dedication
- **Progression Focus**: Encourages specific playstyles
- **Community Events**: Sometimes tied to global objectives

### Loyalty Tiers

Players accumulate loyalty points based on consecutive days played and total matches.

**Loyalty Tier System**:

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Bronze** | 7 consecutive days, 50 total matches | +5% soft currency, 1 weekly raffle ticket |
| **Silver** | 30 consecutive days, 200 total matches | +10% soft currency, 2 weekly raffle tickets, tournament discounts (10%) |
| **Gold** | 90 consecutive days, 500 total matches | +15% soft currency, 3 weekly raffle tickets, tournament discounts (15%), exclusive charms |
| **Platinum** | 180 consecutive days, 1,000 total matches | +20% soft currency, 5 weekly raffle tickets, tournament discounts (20%), priority support |
| **Diamond** | 365 consecutive days, 2,500 total matches | +25% soft currency, 10 weekly raffle tickets, tournament discounts (25%), exclusive access |

**Loyalty Benefits**:
- **Soft Currency Multipliers**: Permanent earning increases
- **Reduced Fees**: Tournament entry discounts
- **Raffle Access**: Automatic entry into exclusive raffles
- **Priority Features**: Early access to new content
- **Cosmetic Rewards**: Loyalty-exclusive items
- **Status Recognition**: Special badges and titles

**Maintaining Loyalty**:
- Daily login sufficient (no matches required)
- Grace periods for missed days (2-day buffer at higher tiers)
- Vacation mode (pause loyalty, available at Gold+)
- Comeback bonuses if streak is lost

<Callout type="warning">
**Streak Protection**: Higher loyalty tiers offer protection against missed days, but maintaining consistency is key to maximum benefits.
</Callout>

### Event Passes

Seasonal events introduce event passes with progressive rewards.

**Event Pass Structure**:
- **Free Tier**: Available to all players, modest rewards
- **Premium Tier**: Optional purchase, enhanced rewards
- **Duration**: Typically 4-8 weeks per season
- **Levels**: 50-100 levels per pass

**Earning Event XP**:
- Playing matches (any mode)
- Completing event-specific quests
- Participating in themed tournaments
- Daily login bonuses
- Special event challenges

**Free Tier Rewards** (Examples):
- Soft currency bundles
- Common charms
- Character XP boosts
- Cosmetic items
- Raffle tickets

**Premium Tier Rewards** (Additional):
- Exclusive characters
- Rare charms
- Unique cosmetics
- Soft currency multipliers
- Event-themed items
- Early level rewards (claim free tier immediately)

**Event Pass Benefits**:
- Seasonal content and themes
- Limited-time exclusives
- Community goals
- Bonus challenges
- Cosmetic collections

## Cross-Player Competitions and Ranking

Competitions extend beyond individual play, fostering community and rivalry.

### Clan Rankings

Clans accumulate points based on member performances in ranked matches and tournaments.

**Clan Point System**:
- **Individual Ranked Wins**: 10-50 points (based on opponent difficulty)
- **Tournament Performance**: 100-5,000 points (based on placement)
- **Clan Wars**: Bonus points for head-to-head victories
- **Activity Bonuses**: Points for clan member participation
- **Special Events**: Bonus points during clan competitions

**Clan Leaderboards**:
- **Global Rankings**: All clans worldwide
- **Regional Rankings**: Area-specific competition
- **Seasonal Resets**: Fresh start each season
- **Historical Records**: Track clan legacy

**Clan Ranking Rewards**:
- **Top 10 Clans**: Significant soft currency for all members, exclusive clan cosmetics
- **Top 100 Clans**: Moderate rewards, clan prestige bonuses
- **Top 1000 Clans**: Small rewards, recognition badges
- **Participation**: All active clans receive baseline rewards

**Clan Benefits**:
- Prestige badges and clan banners
- Soft currency bonuses for members
- Customization options
- Priority tournament access
- Exclusive clan events

### Cross-Region Tournaments

Special events pit regions against one another in friendly competition.

**Regional Competition Format**:
- **Regional Teams**: Players represent their region
- **Qualification**: Top players from each region selected
- **Team Scoring**: Combined performance determines winner
- **Prizes**: Regional pride + rewards for all participants

**Participating Regions**:
- East Asia (China, Japan, Korea)
- Southeast Asia (Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, Philippines)
- North America (USA, Canada)
- Europe (combined or by country for major markets)
- Oceania (Australia, New Zealand)
- Others (growing regions)

**Regional Tournament Benefits**:
- Cultural exchange
- Regional pride and identity
- Massive prize pools
- Broadcast visibility
- Talent discovery

<Callout type="success">
**Global Unity**: Cross-region tournaments celebrate Mahjong as a global game while fostering healthy regional rivalries.
</Callout>

### AI Leaderboards

AI companions have their own ranking system separate from human players.

**AI Performance Metrics**:
- **Win Rate**: Overall success percentage
- **Average Points**: Points earned per match
- **Risk Management**: Balance of aggression and caution
- **Efficiency**: Speed of decision-making
- **Adaptability**: Performance across different opponents

**AI Leaderboard Categories**:
- **Overall AI Rankings**: Best-performing AIs platform-wide
- **Variant-Specific**: Top AIs per Mahjong variant
- **Owner Rankings**: Players with most successful AIs
- **Rental Popularity**: Most rented/requested AIs
- **Improvement Rate**: Fastest-learning AIs

**AI Competition Benefits**:
- Encourages AI training investment
- Refines AI strategies
- Creates AI rental demand
- Showcases training success
- Provides learning benchmarks

**AI Tournaments**:
- AI-only competitions
- Showcase trained AI capabilities
- Prizes for winning AI owners
- Research and development insights
- Entertainment value

## Progression Philosophy

### Balanced Advancement

**Design Principles**:
- **Always Something to Earn**: Multiple progression paths
- **No Dead Ends**: Prestige and seasonal systems prevent caps
- **Skill Matters Most**: Progression accelerates with skill, not just time
- **Accessible to All**: F2P players can fully progress
- **Optional Acceleration**: Premium options available but not required

### Long-Term Engagement

**Retention Strategies**:
- **Daily Reasons to Play**: Quests, bonuses, social activity
- **Weekly Goals**: Challenges and tournaments
- **Monthly Milestones**: Loyalty tiers and seasonal progress
- **Yearly Achievement**: Long-term loyalty recognition
- **Perpetual Goals**: Collection completion, prestige systems

<Callout type="info">
**Endless Journey**: Mahjong Stars' progression systems ensure there's always a next goal, whether you play casually or competitively.
</Callout>
